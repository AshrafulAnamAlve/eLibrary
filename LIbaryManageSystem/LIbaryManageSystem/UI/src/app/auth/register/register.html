Once you create account, approval request will be sent to admin. If that is approved, your account will be created.

You can have maximum 3 books ordered that have not been returned.

You can keep a book with you for maximum 10 days.

If book is not returned after 10 days, you will receive an email.

After 10th day, a fine of 50tk will be imposed every day on each book not returned. This fine will be emailed to you.

If your fine exceeds 500tk or the book is damaged, Admin can block your account.
